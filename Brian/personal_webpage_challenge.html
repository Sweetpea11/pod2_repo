My Drone Life

I started droning about a year ago after seeing numerous articles and shows on tv picturing them. My first flight was at a park near home and I thought I was an expert after that. A couple months later, thinking I was that expert, I was flying near the Colgate clock in Jersey City and miscalculated where I was and flew my drone into the side of the Goldman Sachs Building, crashed the drone and it got stuck 40 floors up. I was never able to recover it.
